About Menafa Hostels

Menafa Hostels was founded to provide students with a safe, affordable, and welcoming home away from home. Our mission is to support your academic journey and well-being, offering a peaceful environment, nutritious meals, and a vibrant community where every student can thrive.

Founded in 2025 by Leonard Lesa and Christine Phiri, Menafa Hostels was built to provide students with a home that is affordable, convenient, and welcoming to all.

We believe in harmony with the environment and our community, offering a peaceful space for learning and growth.

Our commitment is to inclusivity, sustainability, and the well-being of every resident. At Menafa, you are part of a caring family, surrounded by nature and opportunity.
